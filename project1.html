Project Overview.

While interning at Mojo, we did a pitch for a CRM for Falcon Associates. After we won the pitch we scaled the concept up to the whole system.

Process.

Without direct access to the client we went through the client specifications in detail and made a plan with the project manager on how to best approach the pitch.

Pitch.
