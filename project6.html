Groovy Trail.

Introduction.

Groovy Trail is an iPhone app prototype created for the Interaction Design Studio class at CMU. The assignment was to create a levada walk app. A levada is a trail along an irrigation channel or aqueduct, specific to the island of Madeira, Portugal.
